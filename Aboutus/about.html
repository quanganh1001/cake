Introduce

Brand story

Founded in December 2010 from the love of cakes, The Cake started with the slogan "The Cake every day" and the mission throughout to bring the most delicious products. In the early years, The Cake's main products were cakes and fresh bread. In every holiday or birthday, Fresh Garden's cake is always one of the top choices, because of its moderate sweetness, beautiful cakes, and reasonable prices. Besides, customers' daily companions are nutritious and delicious fresh bread products.

Journey mark

With more than 100 stores and distribution agents covering the Northern provinces, The Cake is proud to be the most prestigious brand of fresh cakes with modern advanced production lines and nearly 1000 employees who work non-stop. creative. For fans of sweet, savory, and cream cakes, The Cake is really a "miniature world" of fruit cakes, to please any guest who stops by. After a decade of creative innovations, The Cake is not only marked by quality but also because of the sophistication in each taste, variety of European and Asian cakes and gradually becoming a habit in consumer culture. of cake lovers.

The strength of The Cake lies in the lines of soft, sweet and fresh cream cakes, especially crispy toasted bread, Japanese standard rolls with Hokkaido milk cream ... which are very popular. In every holiday or birthday, The Cake's cake is always a top choice, because of its moderate sweetness, beautiful cake design, and reasonable price. Besides, accompanying customers every day are fresh bread products - convenient and nutritious sandwiches.

Mission and vision

Behind The Cake is a team of staff, expert chefs who always strive to be creative, constantly passionate to be able to offer delicious, quality, beautiful and safe products for customers' health. Going with a strict, closed baking process, requires employees to be careful, meticulous and take care in every smallest stage. The Cake is committed to bringing the highest quality products as a tribute to the love and trust.
